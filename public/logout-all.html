Log out of all usekarma.dev apps

Clicking the button below will open logout pages for all configured *.usekarma.dev apps in new tabs. Each tab runs its own logout flow and then you can close them.

This will log you out from:
  • grafana.usekarma.dev
  • clickhouse.usekarma.dev
  • prometheus.usekarma.dev
  • mongo.usekarma.dev
  • redpanda.usekarma.dev

Your browser may open several tabs. After they finish loading, you should be logged out everywhere. You can then return to the main demo site.
